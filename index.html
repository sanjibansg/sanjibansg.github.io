About Me

Hey there!
This is Sanjiban Sengupta! I primarily work in the domains of Machine Learning and Back-end Development. I love to explore emerging technologies, and play with them. Enthusiastic and believer of how AI can change the world. I am also a huge fan of open-source, and I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills by cotnributing to the community. Apart from technology, I love to act, mostly stage and street plays. I have been appearing on stage since 9 years old, and first appeared in a street play in college during my undergraduate education. Furthermore, I also pursue poetry, and have a particular liking on the Hindi language. I have performed in several open-mic events and have received recognition for my compositions and performances. Before university, I pursued music and fine-art as well, and graduated with Sangeet Bhushan and Chitra Visharad degrees respectively from Pracheen Kala Kendra, Chandigarh.
